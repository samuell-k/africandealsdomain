Return Eligibility

✅ Eligible for Return

  • • Items in original condition
  • • Unused and unopened products
  • • Within 30 days of delivery
  • • Original packaging included
  • • All tags and labels attached

❌ Not Eligible

  • • Used or damaged items
  • • Personal care products
  • • Perishable goods
  • • Custom or personalized items
  • • Digital products or downloads

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Some restrictions apply based on product type and condition.

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account, go to "Orders", select the item you want to return, and click "Return Item".

2

Select Reason

Choose your return reason from the dropdown menu. This helps us improve our products and services.

3

Print Label

Print the prepaid return shipping label and securely package your item with all original packaging.

4

Ship Back

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive tracking updates via email.

5

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-10 business days.

Refund Information

Refund Timeline

  • • Processing: 1-2 business days
  • • Bank transfer: 3-5 business days
  • • Mobile money: 1-2 business days
  • • Credit card: 5-10 business days

Refund Methods

  • • Original payment method
  • • Bank account transfer
  • • Mobile money refund
  • • Store credit (optional)

Important Notes

  • • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • • Shipping costs are not refunded unless the item was defective
  • • International returns may take longer due to customs processing
  • • You'll receive email confirmation when your refund is processed

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. Do not use the item and take photos for documentation.
