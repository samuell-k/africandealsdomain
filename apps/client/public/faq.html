Buying Questions

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in, provide shipping details, and complete payment to confirm your order.

We accept various payment methods including mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets. Payment options may vary by country.

Shipping times vary by location and delivery method. Local deliveries typically take 2-5 business days, while international shipping can take 7-21 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Some restrictions apply. Please check our Returns Policy for details.

Selling Questions

To start selling, create a seller account, complete your profile, and submit required documentation for verification. Once approved, you can list your products and start selling to customers across Africa.

We charge a competitive commission rate of 5-15% depending on your seller tier and product category. There are no upfront fees to start selling. Check our How It Works page for detailed fee structure.

Payments are processed weekly and transferred to your registered bank account or mobile money account. You can track your earnings and payment history in your seller dashboard.

Account Questions

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.

Log into your account, go to "Account Settings" or "Profile", and you can update your personal information, shipping addresses, and payment methods.

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we handle your data.
