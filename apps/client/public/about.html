Our Story

ADD Physical Products was born from a vision to connect Africa's entrepreneurs, artisans, and businesses with buyers across the continent and beyond. We believe in the power of trade to transform lives, create jobs, and build a brighter future for all.

Founded in 2024, we've grown from a small startup to a trusted platform serving thousands of businesses and customers across Rwanda, Kenya, and Nigeria. Our commitment to quality, security, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.
